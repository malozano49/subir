Saved Searches

From this option, you can view the searches you have saved in previous sessions.

  • Searches are displayed in descending order by date.
  • If you want to delete a saved search, click on the option associated with the search you're interested in.

From this option, you can also view the most recent searches you have conducted during the current session. To do this, click on Recent Searches.

  • If you want to save any of the searches, click on the option associated with the search you're interested in.
    • - By default, you can save a maximum of 50 searches.

2. Access to Authority Search

To perform an authority search that allows you to locate records containing a specific authority, click on the Authority Search option.

3. Access to Index Search

To perform a search that locates words or patterns of words similar to the term or terms entered, click on the Index Search option.

4. Access to Favorites

To view the records you have marked as favorites, click on Favorites.

5. Access to My Profile

Click on the My Profile option and identify yourself to access the following ODA utilities:

  • Personal information
  • Requests
  • Active loans
  • Loan history
  • Renew loans
  • Cancel reservations
  • Save searches
  • Create favorite lists
