Favorites

From this option, you can view the records you have marked as favorites.

By default, the search results are displayed in groups of 10, but you have the option to group the results by 25 or 35.

To navigate through the different pages of the search results, you can use the following options:

  • Click on to move to the next page.
  • Click on to move to the previous page.
  • Click on the page number you want to view

  • Click on the number 1 to view the first page

  • Click on the number before "Next" to go to the last page

How to Move a Favorite to Another Existing List

If you want to move one of the records you have in one list to a different one, you need to identify yourself first. To do this, click on

  • 1. Click on your user profile and select the "My Lists" option.
  • 2. Click on the list where the record you want to move is located.
  • 3. Click on the option associated with the record you want to change to another list.
  • 4. Select the list to which you want to move the record
  • 5. Click on to move the record to the selected list.

How to Delete Records from Existing Lists

  • 2. Click on the list where the record you want to delete is located
  • 3. Click on the option associated with the record you want to delete

Access to Authority Search

To perform an authority search that allows you to locate records containing a specific authority, click on the Authority Search option.

Access to Index Search

To perform a search that locates words or patterns of words similar to the term or terms entered, click on the Index Search option.

Access to Recently Conducted Searches

To view the 10 most recent searches that returned results during the OPAC session, click on Recent Searches.

Access to Saved Searches

If you want to save any of the searches you've conducted to run them at another time, click on Saved Searches.

* To save searches, it is necessary to identify yourself beforehand.

Access to My Profile

Click on the My Profile option and identify yourself to access the following ODA utilities:

  • Personal information
  • Requests
  • Active loans
  • Loan history
  • Renew loans
  • Cancel reservations
  • Save searches
  • Create favorite lists

To identify yourself as a user, you can use any of these 3 methods:

  • Library card number
  • Email provided to your library
  • Mobile phone number provided to your library

Access to the Home Page

To go to the home page, click on Home.
