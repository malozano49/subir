Recent Searches

From this option, you can view the last 5 searches you have performed.

  • To view the complete list of recent searches, click on
  • Searches are displayed in descending mode
  • The following information related to the search is displayed:
    • the performed search
    • the selected data source
    • the sub-catalog used
  • To execute any of the searches, click on the one you are interested in, and the located records will be displayed in list mode.
  • If you want to save any of the searches, identify yourself and click on the option associated with the search of interest.
    • by default, you can store a maximum of 50 searches

Access to Authority Search

To perform an authority search that allows you to locate records containing a specific authority, click on the Authority Search option.

Access to Index Search

To perform a search that locates words or patterns of words similar to the term or terms entered, click on the Index Search option.

Access to Favorites

To view the records you have marked as favorites, click on Favorites.

Access to My Profile

Click on the My Profile option and identify yourself to access the following ODA utilities:

  • Personal information
  • Requests
  • Active loans
  • Loan history
  • Renew loans
  • Cancel reservations
  • Save searches
  • Create favorites lists
