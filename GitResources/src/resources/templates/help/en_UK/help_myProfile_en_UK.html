How to modify personal data

  • 1. Click on to modify the data you are interested in
  • 2. Make the desired changes
  • 3. Click on to save the modifications

How to view active loans

  • 1. Click on to view the list of your active loans
    • The following loan-related information is displayed:
      • Library of the item
      • Branch of the request
      • Title
      • Author
      • Date of loan
      • Date of return
      • The option if the request is an efilm content
    • Loans are displayed sorted by the return date in ascending order
    • If you click on any of the loans, the associated title's details are displayed

How to Navigate the List of Active Loans

By default, search results are displayed in groups of 10. However, you have the option to group results by 25 or 35.

To navigate through different pages of the obtained results, you have the following options:

  • Click on to move to the next page.
  • Click on to move to the previous page.
  • Click on the page number you want to view.
  • Click on the number 1 to view the first page.
  • Click on the number before "Next" to go to the last page.

How to View Requests

  • Click on
    • The following data related to pending requests are displayed:
      • Library of the item
      • Branch of the request
      • Title
      • Author
      • Creation date
      • Completion date
      • Counter
      • Information about its status
    • Requests are displayed grouped by Library/Branch sorted by completion date in ascending order.
    • If you click on any of the requests, the details of the associated title are displayed.

    How to Cancel a Reservation Request

    • 1. Click on
    • 2. Click on the option associated with the reservation you want to cancel.

      - A confirmation message will appear.

    • 3. Click on the option to delete the request.
    You can only cancel reservations that are not activated, meaning they are not yet available for pickup.

    How to Cancel a Loan Request

    • 1. Click on
    • 2. Click on the option associated with the request you want to cancel.

      - A confirmation message will appear.

    • 3. Click on the option to delete the request.
    You can only cancel requests that are in the R-Request status.

    How to Renew Loans

    1. Click on

    2. Click on the option associated with the loan you want to renew.

    Access to the Homepage

    To go to the homepage, click on Home.

    Access to Authority Search

    To perform an authority search that allows you to locate records containing a specific authority, click on the Authority Search option.

    Access to Index Search

    To perform a search that locates words or word patterns similar to the entered term or terms, click on the Index Search option.

    Access to Recently Performed Searches

    To view the last 10 searches that returned results during the Opac session, click on Recent Searches.

    Access to Saved Searches

    If you want to save any of the searches you've performed to execute them later, click on Saved Searches.

    * To save searches, you need to be identified in advance.

    Access to Favorites

    To view the records you've marked as favorites, click on Favorites.
