Viewing in Detail Mode

Detail mode displays the following information:

  • - Cover
  • - Title
  • - Author
  • - Publication
  • - Summary
  • - Genre
  • - Material Type
  • - Information about copies associated with the record:
    • o Location
    • o Total number of copies associated with the record
    • o Number of available copies
  • If you want to see information from other fields included in the record, click on the option in the floating bar

    • To enable the MARC format, click on

  • * If other matching records are found in certain fields with the currently viewed record, they are displayed at the bottom in a carousel format.

    To access other related records, click on the option in the floating bar.

    The fields considered for records to be related to the currently viewed one are as follows:

    • - Same publisher and collection
    • - Same genre

How to Mark the Currently Viewed Record as a Favorite

To mark the currently viewed record as a favorite, click on the option.

If you want to view the records marked as favorites in the current session, click on Favorites.

  • By default, you can store a maximum of 100 favorites
  • Favorites are displayed in order by author/title
  • To unmark a favorite, click on the option for the record you want to remove from favorites.
    • - Records can also be unmarked from list or detail view

To add the record to one of the already created favorites lists, follow these steps:

  • 1. Click on the option associated with the record.
  • 2. Select the list you are interested in.

  • 3. Click .

    • Records belonging to a favorites list are displayed with the following distinctive image:

    • The same record can be associated with multiple favorites lists.

How to Navigate the List of Copies

By default, copies are displayed in groups of 10. To navigate through the different pages of the results, you have the following options:

  • Click on to move to the next page.
  • Click on to move to the previous page.
  • Click on the page number you want to view

  • Click on number 1 to view the first page

  • Click on the number just before Next to go to the last page

Access to Results List

If you want to go back and view all the records found in the search, click on the option Results List.

Access to Authority Search

To perform an authority search to locate records that contain a specific authority, click on Authority Search.

Access to Index Search

To perform a search that locates words or word patterns similar to the term or terms entered, click on Index Search.

Access to Recently Performed Searches

To view the 10 most recent searches that returned results during the Opac session, click on Recently Performed Searches.

Access to Saved Searches

If you want to save any of the searches you've conducted for future use, click on Saved Searches.

  • In order to save searches, you need to identify yourself in advance.

Access to Favorites

To view the records you've marked as favorites, click on Favorites.
