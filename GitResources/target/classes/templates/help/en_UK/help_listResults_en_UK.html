View in List Mode

By default, search results are displayed in list mode.

By default, the following information is shown in list mode:

  • Cover
  • Author
  • Title
  • Publication
  • ISBN/ISSN
  • Material Type
  • Information about the copies associated with the record:
    • - Location
    • - Total number of copies associated with the record
    • - Number of available copies

How to Sort Search Results

Records are displayed by default in Author/Title order.

Other sorting options include:

  • 1. Relevance
  • 2. Title
  • 3. Publication Date:

    When sorting by publication date, records are displayed in descending order.

How to Navigate Through the List of Results in the Search

By default, search results are displayed in groups of 10, but you can group the results by 25 or 35.

To navigate through the different pages of the results, you have the following options:

  • Click on to go to the next page.
  • Click on to go to the previous page.
  • Click on the page number you want to view

  • Click on the number 1 to view the first page

  • Click on the number before Next to go to the last page

How to Mark Records as Favorites in the Search

To mark any of the records located in the search as favorites, click on the option associated with the record you want to add as a favorite.

If you want to view the records marked as favorites in the current session, click on Favorites.

  • By default, you can store a maximum of 100 favorites.
  • Favorites are displayed in order of author/title.
  • To unmark a favorite, click on the option of the record you want to remove from favorites.
    • - Records can also be unmarked from list view or detail view.

Access to Complete Record View

To view a record located in the search results in detailed mode, click on it.

Access to Authority Search

To perform an authority search that allows you to locate records containing a specific authority, click on the "Authority Search" option.

Access to Index Search

To perform a search that locates words or word patterns similar to the entered term or terms, click on the "Index Search" option.

Access to Recent Searches

To view the last 10 searches that have returned results during the current OPAC session, click on "Recent Searches."

Access to Saved Searches

If you want to save any of the searches you have performed to execute them at another time, click on "Saved Searches."

  • To save searches, you need to identify yourself beforehand.

Access to Favorites

To view the records you have marked as favorites, click on "Favorites."

Access to My Profile

Click on the "My Profile" option and identify yourself to access the following ODA utilities:

  • Personal Information
  • Requests
  • Active Loans
  • Loan History
  • Renew Loans
  • Cancel Reservations
  • Save Searches
  • Create Favorites Lists

• To identify yourself as a user, you can use any of these 3 methods:

  • Library card number
  • Email address provided to your library
  • Mobile phone number provided to your library

Access to the Homepage

To go to the homepage, click on "Home."
